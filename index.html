Welcome to Song Association Game!

Sing songs containing a random word in English or Malay. Choose Regular Mode for competitive play with scores, Casual Mode for practice, or Gauntlet Mode for timed team challenges. Works offline after initial load!

Casual Mode: Practice without scores.
